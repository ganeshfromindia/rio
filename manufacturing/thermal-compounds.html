Our Products

We have designed various grades of thermal compunds applications where quantum of heat flow is variable. thermal compunds manufactured by us never cures irrespective of heat produced in the system. We have designed various grades of thermal compunds depending upon end application. We can design & manufacture thermal compundss as per customer’s specific demand based on heat dissipation requirement.
